Product Help

Vegetable-tanned leather can grow and shrink due to various environmental factors like humidity. As humidity levels change throughout the year, some warping of your Leather Desk Pad or Mouse Pad is normal and to be expected. Some people have found that it helps to use a leather conditioner, so you can consider finding one that can be used with vegetable-tanned leather and follow the directions for use. Please note that some leather conditioners can alter the finish of your Leather Desk Pad or Mouse Pad.
